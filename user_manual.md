# User Manual - Greenwood School Website

## Section 1: For Parents and Visitors

### How to Navigate the Website

1. **Homepage:** Landing page with school overview, stats, and latest events
2. **Navigation Bar:** Click links at the top to navigate to different sections
3. **Mobile Menu:** On mobile devices, tap the hamburger menu (☰) to see navigation options

### How to Complete Student Registration

**Step-by-Step Guide:**

1. Click "Register Now" button in the navigation bar or go to `/registration`
2. Fill in all required fields marked with asterisk (*):
   - Student Name (minimum 2 characters)
   - Date of Birth (select from calendar)
   - Class Applying For (select from dropdown)
   - Gender (select Male, Female, or Other)
   - Parent/Guardian Name (minimum 2 characters)
   - Phone Number (exactly 10 digits)
   - Email Address (valid email format)
   - Home Address (minimum 10 characters)
   - Previous School (optional)
3. Click "Submit Registration" button
4. Wait for success confirmation message
5. You will be contacted within 48 hours regarding the admission process

**Registration Status Meanings:**
- **Pending:** Your application is under review
- **Approved:** Your child has been accepted for admission
- **Rejected:** Unfortunately, admission could not be granted at this time

### How to Contact the School

1. Navigate to the "Contact" page
2. Fill in the contact form with your details
3. Type your message in the message box
4. Click "Send Message"
5. You will receive a confirmation that your message has been sent

**Alternative Contact Methods:**
- **Phone:** +91 80 2345 6789
- **Email:** info@greenwoodschool.edu.in
- **Address:** 123 Education Lane, Greenwood Nagar, Bangalore - 560001
- **Office Hours:** Monday - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

## Section 2: For Admin Staff

### How to Login to Admin Dashboard

1. Navigate to `/admin/login`
2. Enter your email: `admin@greenwood.edu`
3. Enter your password: `Admin@1234`
4. Click "Login" button
5. You will be redirected to the dashboard

### How to Manage Registrations

**View All Registrations:**
1. Login to admin dashboard
2. Click "Registrations" in the sidebar
3. View all registration requests in the table

**Approve Registration:**
1. Go to Registrations page
2. Find the registration you want to approve
3. Click the ✓ (checkmark) button
4. Status will change to "Approved"

**Reject Registration:**
1. Go to Registrations page
2. Find the registration you want to reject
3. Click the ✗ (cross) button
4. Status will change to "Rejected"

**Delete Registration:**
1. Go to Registrations page
2. Find the registration you want to delete
3. Click the 🗑 (trash) button
4. Confirm deletion when prompted

**Search Registrations:**
1. Use the search box at the top
2. Type student name, email, or status
3. Table will filter automatically

**Export Registrations as CSV:**
1. Go to Registrations page
2. Click "Export CSV" button
3. File will download automatically

### How to Add and Manage Students

**Add New Student:**
1. Login to admin dashboard
2. Click "Students" in the sidebar
3. Click "Add Student" button
4. Fill in the form:
   - Name, Class, Section, Roll Number
   - Parent Name, Phone
5. Click "Save"

**Edit Student:**
1. Go to Students page
2. Click the ✎ (edit) button next to the student
3. Update the information
4. Click "Save"

**Delete Student:**
1. Go to Students page
2. Click the 🗑 (trash) button
3. Confirm deletion

### How to Create School Events

## Section 3: Troubleshooting

### Cannot Login

**Problem:** Unable to login to admin dashboard  
**Solution:**
- Check caps lock is off
- Verify email and password are correct
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Contact technical support if issue persists

### Form Not Submitting

**Problem:** Registration or contact form not submitting  
**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Check for error messages in red
- Try refreshing the page and try again

### Page Not Loading

**Problem:** Pages not loading or showing errors  
**Solution:**
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Contact technical support if issue persists

### Images Not Displaying

**Problem:** Images not showing on gallery or pages  
**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache

## Section 4: FAQ

**Q1: How do I register my child for admission?**  
A: Click the "Register Now" button, fill the registration form, and submit. You'll be contacted within 48 hours.

**Q2: What is the fee structure?**  
A: Visit the "Admissions" page to view the complete fee structure for all classes.

**Q3: How can I check my registration status?**  
A: Currently, status updates are communicated via phone or email. Future updates may include online status checking.

**Q4: What documents are required for admission?**  
A: Birth certificate, transfer certificate, previous year report card, passport photos, Aadhar card, parent ID proof, address proof, and medical certificate.

**Q5: Is there an entrance test?**  
A: Yes, for Class 6 and above, an entrance assessment is conducted.

**Q6: What are the school timings?**  
A: Office hours are Monday - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Class timings are provided during admission.

**Q7: How do I contact the school?**  
A: You can call +91 80 2345 6789, email info@greenwoodschool.edu.in, or visit the school at 123 Education Lane, Greenwood Nagar, Bangalore - 560001.

**Q8: Is transportation available?**  
A: Yes, transportation is available for an additional fee. Details are provided in the fee structure.

**Q9: What curriculum does the school follow?**  
A: Greenwood School follows the CBSE curriculum.

**Q10: How can I reset my admin password?**  
A: Contact the technical team or database administrator to reset the password in the database.
